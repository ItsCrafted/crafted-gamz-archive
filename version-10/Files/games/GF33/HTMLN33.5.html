Crafted Gamz Tips & Tricks

Top Tips to Enhance Your Experience:

  • Track Your Time: On "Total Time With Crafted Gamz In Miscellaneous", you can view the total time you've ever spent on Crafted Gamz version IX and up!
  • Send Feedback: Send feedback through the forums in Miscellaneous to help improve the Crafted Gamz experience.
  • Customize Your Experience: Change your background image and theme in the settings to make Crafted Gamz truly your own!
  • Join the Google Chats: If you haven't already, join the Google chats to stay connected with other users and the Crafted Gamz community.
